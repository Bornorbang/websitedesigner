FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What Our Clients Want to Know

How quickly can you build my website? +

Great question! The timeline really depends on what you're looking for. If you need a simple, clean website with a few pages, we can usually have you up and running in 1-3 weeks. For something more complex like an online store or a custom platform with special features, we're looking at about 6-12 weeks. We take our time to make sure everything is perfect, properly tested, and optimized for your success.

What will my website cost? +

We totally understand you want to know the investment upfront! Our pricing varies based on what you need - think of it like building a house, the cost depends on the size and features you want. We work with different budgets and always aim to give you the best value. Plus, we run special discounts throughout the year! Check out our pricing page for details, or just message us for a personalized quote that fits your budget.

What kind of websites do you specialize in? +

We love variety! We've built everything from sleek corporate sites to bustling online stores, educational platforms, real estate websites, fintech solutions, investment platforms, banking portals, and so much more. Honestly, if you can dream it, we can probably build it. Every business is unique, and we create websites that perfectly match your vision and goals.

Do you offer other services besides web design? +

Absolutely! We're like a one-stop digital shop. Beyond creating beautiful websites, we also help with SEO to get you found on Google, graphic design for your branding needs, mobile app development, social media management, and ongoing content management. We believe in supporting your entire digital journey, not just building your website and disappearing.

Will you help me after my website goes live? +

Of course! We don't just build and run. You get a full month of free technical support and maintenance after launch - think of it as our way of making sure everything runs smoothly while you get comfortable with your new site. After that first month, we offer flexible maintenance plans so you can choose the level of ongoing support that works for your business.

What do you need from me to get started? +

To get started, we’ll need details about your business operations, content (text, images), and any design preferences you have. If you already have a domain and hosting, great! If not, we can assist you purchase them. Once we have these, we’ll begin the development process right away!

What else do I need to know? +

We’re committed to making your website a success. This means we’ll need your feedback and input throughout the process. We’ll also provide training on how to use your new site, so you feel confident managing it. And remember, our support doesn’t end at launch - we’re here for you as your business grows and evolves! One more thing, please check out our terms of service.
