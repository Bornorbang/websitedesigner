Social Media Management FAQs

Which social media platforms do you manage? +

We manage all major social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter (X), LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, and Pinterest. The number of platforms included depends on your chosen package. We'll help you select the most effective platforms for your target audience and business goals!

What's included in content creation? +

Our content creation includes custom-designed graphics, engaging captions, relevant hashtags, and scheduling. Professional and Enterprise packages also include video content, GIFs, carousel posts, and stories. We align all content with your brand voice and marketing objectives to maximize engagement and reach!

Do you handle customer responses and messages? +

Yes! Community management is included in all packages. We monitor comments, respond to messages, engage with your audience, and maintain a positive brand presence. The Enterprise package includes 24/7 monitoring to ensure no customer inquiry goes unanswered. We'll keep your community active and engaged!

Are paid ads included in the pricing? +

Only the Enterprise package includes paid ads management (your ad budget is separate). We handle campaign strategy, ad creation, targeting, optimization, and performance tracking. The Starter and Professional packages focus on organic growth, but we can add paid ads management as an extra service. Contact us for custom ad budget recommendations!

How do you measure success and ROI? +

We provide monthly analytics reports covering key metrics like reach, engagement rate, follower growth, website clicks, conversions, and more. Professional and Enterprise packages receive detailed insights with competitor analysis and strategic recommendations. We focus on metrics that matter to your business goals and demonstrate clear ROI!

Can I approve content before it's posted? +

Absolutely! We share a content calendar with you at the beginning of each month for review and approval. You can request revisions or provide feedback on any content. Once approved, we schedule and publish the posts. This ensures complete transparency and alignment with your brand standards!

What other digital services do you offer? +

Beyond social media management, we offer Web Development, Shopify Store Design, SEO Services, Email Marketing, and Graphic Design. We're a full-service digital agency that can handle all your online marketing needs. Contact us for integrated marketing solutions!
